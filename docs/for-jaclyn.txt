WHAT YOUR ROLE ACTUALLY IS
----------------------------
Your core job is the Health Check review. That is the center of this
business. Everything else flows from that.

Specifically, during a Health Check you are looking for:

  FORMULA RISKS
  - Formulas that reference other tabs or files (if those move, it breaks)
  - Circular references
  - Hardcoded values where a formula should be
  - VLOOKUP or INDEX/MATCH pointing to ranges that could shift
  - Division by zero risks

  DATA RISKS
  - No data validation (anyone can type anything anywhere)
  - Duplicate entries
  - Inconsistent formatting (dates stored as text, numbers stored as text)
  - Mixed data types in the same column

  PROCESS RISKS
  - Manual steps that depend on one person (copy/paste from email,
    manual entry every Monday, etc.)
  - No version control (multiple copies floating around)
  - No backup process
  - Single-user dependency (only one person knows the system)

  AUDIT RISKS
  - Records that could be questioned in a compliance review
  - Data that cannot be traced back to a source
  - Calculations that cannot be independently verified

  TRIBAL KNOWLEDGE
  - Anything that only works because the person who built it is there
  - Undocumented rules ("don't touch column G")
  - Hidden sheets or named ranges that the client does not know exist

You write up what you find. Landon formats the report. The client
gets a document they can act on.
